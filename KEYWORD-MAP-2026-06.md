# I Care Air Care - Service Page Keyword Map (2026-06)

**Purpose:** one owner keyword per service page (kills cannibalization), with secondaries, GSC proof, new title/meta, and internal-link plan. Drives the rebuild of all 13 service pages using the `ac-repair-tampa` model.

**Sources merged:** GSC CSV 2026-06-11 (real demand in our market = primary truth) + agency Local SEO Workbook v5.1 (core services only) + client 13x11 tool list (brands/variants only, after filtering).

**Rules applied:** ZERO em-dashes. Honest claims only (4.9 stars / 700+ reviews, license CAC1816515, 1-yr warranty, same-day when available, 0% Synchrony financing). NOT 24/7. Title target ~55-60 chars, meta ~150-155 chars, keyword-first.

**Scope decision (client, 2026-06-11):** strict. Discarded out-of-scope keyword groups: water heater, pool heater, dryer vent cleaning, residential appliance/freezer repair, and all product/DIY terms (buy nest thermostat, levoit air purifier, honeywell battery, etc.). These would dilute topical focus and attract zero qualified local buyers.

---

## Why local exact-match is NOT the target (the core insight)

All three sources agree: hyper-local exact strings have tiny volume. The prize is "near me" + generic terms, won by topical depth + local relevance + Google Business Profile, NOT keyword stuffing.

| Keyword | Real volume (agency Raw Data) |
|---|---|
| ac repair near me | 368,000 |
| ac installation near me | 90,500 |
| hvac repair near me | 60,500 |
| ac repair wesley chapel / wesley chapel ac repair | 480 |
| hvac repair wesley chapel | 30 |
| most hyper-local permutations | 0 |

GSC confirms it: we already rank pos 8-10 on several "near me" terms but the Local Pack eats the click. So content targets the natural query language ("ac repair near me", "emergency ac repair"), and the geo lives in supporting copy, not in 50 repeated city strings.
